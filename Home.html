Hey! Say! JUMP!

Hey! Say! JUMP! is an eight member boy band under the talent agency Johnny & Associates.

The group was assembled in 2007 and has been active ever since.

The name Hey! Say! refers to the time period the members were born in (Heisei) and JUMP is an acronym for Johnny's Ultra Music Power.
