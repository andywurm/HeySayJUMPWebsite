TV Programs

Itadaki High Jump

いただきハイジャンプ

Members That Appear:   All

Description:   Itadaki High Jump is a Japanese variety television show. The main concept is for the band members to solve the worlds "serious problems." For example, helping kids overcome their worst fears, trying to eat a gigantic portion of food (Haikaropa), attempting to beat a pro by violating the rules / cheating, and even determining whether rumors are true or false (Mayutsuba) submitted by their fans!
This program premieres every Saturday at 10:25 a.m. JST on FUJI Television Network.

  School Kakumei!

        スクール革命!

Members That Appear:   Yamada Ryosuke, Yaotome Hikaru, Chinen Yuri

Description: As the title suggests, School Kakumei is a Japanese variety show that has an academic theme. The stage is modeled after a classroom for a year three class. Uchimura Teruyoshi plays the homeroom teacher, the other regular performers (such as the members of Hey! Say! JUMP!) are the students, and the guest talents and intellectuals are the lecturers. Tune in every week to learn new and valuable information while still getting a laugh out of it.
This program premieres every Sunday at 11:45 a.m. JST on Nippon Television Network.

    Hirunandesu!

        ヒルナンデス!

Members That Appear:   Arioka Daiki, Yaotome Hikaru

Description:   Hirunandesu is a Japanese variety show that gives people profitable, interesting and practical information on delicacies, fashion, popular places around Japan. The show features a series of regular members who only appear on a particular day of the week. To see our members please watch on Tuesdays.
This program premieres every Monday to Friday at 11:55 a.m. JST on Nippon Television Network.

    Mezamashi TV

      めざましテレビ

Members That Appear:   Inoo Kei

Description:   Mezamashi TV is a Japanese morning news / talk show broadcast. Aside from the news there also many interesting segments! From checking out local places in Japan to going overseas to get the inside scoop on world event. Especially special reports and interviews conducted our very own Inoo Kei. Be sure to check him out every thursday and lend him your support!
This program premieres every Monday to Friday at 5:25 a.m. JST on FUJI Television Network.
